## Accessibility Notes

By default, `ACiscoLoader`, is assigned the [WAI-ARIA](https://www.w3.org/WAI/standards-guidelines/aria/) role of [progressbar](https://www.w3.org/TR/wai-aria/#progressbar).
Additionally applied are the suggested `aria-valuemin` and `aria-valuemax` set at `0` and `100`, respectively, although all loaders are indeterminate.
